## What this kit is

This is a public-safe proof kit for OntoGuard's evolved Decision Authorization Packet surface. It is designed for website posting, GTM conversations, partner diligence, and buyer education without disclosing source code, protected implementation logic, private ontology internals, raw legal registries, or customer data.

OntoGuard is not presented here as generic AI governance software. The kit demonstrates two connected commercial wedges:

1. **AI Output Risk Review** - identify risky, unsupported, review-heavy, or inadmissible AI outputs before they enter a business workflow.
2. **Decision Authorization Proof** - prove that a proposed AI-driven movement was governed before release, refused when release conditions failed, and prevented from forming a protected downstream effect in a controlled harness.

## Important boundaries

This kit intentionally does not claim production route non-bypassability. It proves controlled-harness enforcement only. Production non-bypassability requires attached production integration evidence.

This kit also does not claim the governed AI output was safe for autonomous release. The point of the sample is the opposite: OntoGuard withheld autonomous release, routed the output to human review, generated no-bind proof, and preserved the decision as a review-gated improvement signal.
